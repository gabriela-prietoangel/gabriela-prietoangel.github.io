Blackletter Scrabble

"Blackletter Scrabble", Blackletter typeface on a wooden cube, creation of a Rotunda font for the letters e, c, t, s. The Rotunda is a sub-category of the Blackletter typeface originated in Germany during the High Middle Ages. "Blackletter Scrabble" is a collaborative project with the aim of creating a multitude of scrabble pieces with different typefaces.
